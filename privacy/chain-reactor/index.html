1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Chain Reactor (“the App”), developed by Orçun Güler (“we”, “us”, or “our”), collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information (Optional)

Users may optionally sign in using third-party authentication providers such as Google or Apple. When you do so, we may receive basic account information such as:

  • User ID
  • Email address (if provided by the platform)

This information is used solely for:

  • Saving game progress
  • Synchronizing purchases
  • Restoring access across devices

2.2 Usage Data (Analytics)

We collect anonymous usage data to improve gameplay and performance. This may include:

  • Gameplay events
  • Session duration
  • Device type and OS version
  • In-app interactions

This data is collected via analytics tools such as Amplitude and does not personally identify you.

2.3 Advertising Data

The App uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising IDs
  • Interaction data with ads

This information may be used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your region and consent.

2.4 In-App Purchases

The App uses third-party services such as RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases.

We do not directly collect or store payment information. All purchases are processed securely through platform providers (Apple App Store / Google Play).

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Analyze usage and performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Deliver advertisements
  • Enable in-app purchases and restore transactions

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Amplitude)
  • Advertising partners (e.g., Google AdMob)
  • Payment and purchase platforms (e.g., Apple, Google, RevenueCat)

These third parties process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing the App’s functionality
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Resolving disputes

Anonymous analytics data may be retained for longer periods.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising

You can:

  • Reset your advertising ID in device settings
  • Manage tracking permissions (iOS App Tracking Transparency)

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us to remove it.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.
