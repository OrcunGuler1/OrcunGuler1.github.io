Circuit Connect

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Circuit Connect is designed to work without requiring account creation. We do not run first-party analytics that profile you for product tracking.

  • If you email support, we receive the information you include (such as your email and message) and use it only to respond.
  • If ads are enabled, advertising partners may process device/app information, ad delivery data, and identifiers required for fraud prevention and ad serving.
  • Gameplay progress, settings, and unlocks are stored locally on your device unless a future in-app feature explicitly says otherwise.

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared only with ad partners as needed for ads, or when required by law.

You can clear local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage. Ad personalization controls are available through your device/platform settings where supported.

Bureau of Lost Minutes

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

We do not sell personal information. Third-party services process data only for ad and purchase functionality under their own terms and privacy policies.
