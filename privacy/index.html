Circuit Connect

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

This policy explains how Circuit Connect handles information when you use the app.

1) Data We Collect

  • Support contact data: if you email support, we receive the information you include in your message.
  • On-device gameplay data: progress, settings, and unlocks are stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising and purchase service data: integrated partners may process technical and transaction metadata required to serve ads and validate purchases.

2) Advertising and In-App Purchases

  • The app supports ads.
  • The app may support optional in-app purchases and subscriptions, handled by Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • Ad and purchase providers may process device/app identifiers, ad events, entitlement status, and fraud-prevention signals under their own policies.

3) No First-Party Tracking

We do not run first-party behavioral tracking or analytics pipelines to profile users across apps or websites.

4) Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with service providers required for ads, purchases, and legal compliance. Support emails are retained only as long as needed to resolve your request.

5) Your Choices and Contact

You can remove local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage. For support or privacy requests, contact orcunguler381@gmail.com.
